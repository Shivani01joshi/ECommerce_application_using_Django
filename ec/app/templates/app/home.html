Dodla Dairy offers a diverse range of products, including various forms of milk, curd, butter, ghee, paneer, cheese, flavored milk, ice cream, and other dairy-based beverages. Known for its high-quality standards, Dodla Dairy ensures rigorous testing and compliance with national and international food safety certifications. The company operates several advanced processing plants and has an extensive distribution network that makes its products accessible in both urban and rural markets. Dodla Dairy is committed to farmer welfare, providing training, veterinary services, and financial assistance to its network of milk suppliers.
